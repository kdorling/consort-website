Submit Your Event

Community organizations and groups are invited to submit their events for inclusion on the Local Groups Calendar.

Submission Guidelines:

  • Events must be community-focused and open to residents
  • Submit event details at least 2 weeks in advance
  • Include event name, date, time, location, and description
  • Provide contact information for inquiries
  • Events are subject to approval
