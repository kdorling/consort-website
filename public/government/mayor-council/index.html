How to Contact Council

You can reach out to any member of Council through:

  • Email: Use the individual email addresses listed above, or send a general inquiry to info@consort.ca
  • Phone: Call the Village Office at [Phone Number] during office hours (weekdays 8:00 AM - 4:30 PM)
  • Mail: Village of Consort, [Address], Consort, AB [Postal Code]
  • In Person: Visit the Village Office during regular business hours

Council members are here to listen to your concerns, answer questions, and represent your interests in local government decisions.

Council Remuneration

In accordance with provincial legislation and Village policy, Council remuneration information is available in the Village’s annual financial statements. This information is posted under the Administration Services transparency section.

Code of Conduct

All members of Council adhere to a Code of Conduct that outlines ethical standards and expectations for elected officials. This ensures accountability, integrity, and respectful interaction with residents, staff, and each other.
