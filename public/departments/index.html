Departments & Services

The Village of Consort provides a full range of municipal services to support our residents, businesses, and visitors. Our dedicated team works every day to maintain infrastructure, deliver programs, ensure public safety, and foster a vibrant, connected community.

Community Services

The Community Services department supports the well-being and vibrancy of Consort by working alongside local organizations to deliver programs and services for residents of all ages. We help coordinate and promote seniors’ programs, youth initiatives, volunteer opportunities, and social support services offered throughout the community. Whether you’re looking to get involved, find support, or connect with others, Community Services is here to help build a caring and inclusive village.

Economic Development

Consort Economic Development is focused on supporting local businesses, attracting new investment, and helping Consort grow in a sustainable and community-minded way. We work to create opportunities for entrepreneurs, promote tourism, and strengthen partnerships that benefit the local economy. Whether you’re starting a business, exploring development options, or looking to invest in our region, we’re here to help you succeed in Consort.

Municipal Services & Utilities

The Municipal Services & Utilities department plays a vital role in maintaining the infrastructure and essential services that keep the Village of Consort safe, clean, and connected. Our team manages sidewalks, water and sewer systems, garbage collection, and road maintenance - including snow removal, street sweeping, line painting, and pothole repairs. Protective Services

The Protective Services department helps keep the Village of Consort safe, prepared, and well-protected. This includes our dedicated Fire Department, which provides emergency response and fire prevention services, as well as our Emergency Management team, which coordinates preparedness planning and response efforts for natural or man-made events. Protective Services also oversees Bylaw Enforcement, working to ensure community standards are upheld with fairness and respect. Together, these services help create a safe and welcoming environment for all residents.
