Departments & Services

The Village of Consort provides a full range of municipal services to support our residents, businesses, and visitors. Our dedicated team works every day to maintain infrastructure, deliver programs, ensure public safety, and foster a vibrant, connected community.

Our Departments

Administration Services

The Administration Services team is your primary contact point for Village operations. Located at the Village Office, our staff handle property tax and utility payments, customer service inquiries, records management, financial services, and support for Council operations.

Office Hours: Weekdays 8:00 AM to 4:30 PM

Protective Services

The Protective Services department helps keep the Village of Consort safe, prepared, and well-protected. This includes our dedicated Fire Department, which provides emergency response and fire prevention services, as well as our Emergency Management team, which coordinates preparedness planning and response efforts for natural or man-made events.
