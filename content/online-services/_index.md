## Frequently Asked Questions

### When will online payments be available?

We're working to implement secure online payment options and will announce the launch date when it's available. In the meantime, please use our current payment options.

### Will there be a fee for using online services?

Most online services will be free. Credit card payments may have a small processing fee. We'll provide clear information about any fees before you complete a transaction.

### Will I need to create an account?

For some services (like bill payments and account management), you'll need to create a secure account. Other services (like service requests) may not require an account.

### What if I don't have internet access?

All current in-person, phone, and mail services will remain available. We'll continue to serve residents through multiple channels.

### Is online payment secure?

Yes. We'll use industry-standard security measures and encryption to protect your financial and personal information.

### Can I still visit the office in person?

Absolutely! The Village Office will continue to provide in-person service. Online services are an additional option for convenience.
