## Frequently Asked Questions

### What are your office hours?

The Village Office is open Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM to 4:30 PM. We're closed on statutory holidays.

### How quickly will I receive a response?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 1-2 business days. Urgent matters are prioritized.

### Can I make an appointment to meet with staff or Council?

Yes! Contact the Village Office to schedule an appointment with staff or to arrange a meeting with the Mayor or a Councillor.

### Do I need to live in Consort to contact you?

No. We welcome inquiries from non-residents, businesses, visitors, and anyone interested in learning more about Consort.

### How do I request to speak at a Council meeting?

Contact the Village Office at least 5 business days before the meeting to request delegation status. See our [Council Meetings page](/government/meetings) for more information.

### Can I remain anonymous when reporting a bylaw concern?

You can request confidentiality when filing a bylaw complaint. Contact information is helpful for follow-up but can remain confidential.
