The Village of Consort provides a full range of municipal services to support our residents, businesses, and visitors. Our dedicated team works every day to maintain infrastructure, deliver programs, ensure public safety, and foster a vibrant, connected community.

## Our Departments

### Administration Services

The Administration Services team is your primary contact point for Village operations. Located at the Village Office, our staff handle property tax and utility payments, customer service inquiries, records management, financial services, and support for Council operations.

**Contact**: info@consort.ca  
**Office Hours**: Weekdays 8:00 AM to 4:30 PM

[Learn More →](/departments/administration)

### Municipal Services & Utilities

Our Municipal Services & Utilities department maintains the essential infrastructure that keeps Consort running smoothly. From water and sewer systems to garbage collection, road maintenance, snow removal, and street care, this team ensures reliable service delivery year-round.

**Contact**: pw@consort.ca

[Learn More →](/departments/municipal-services)

### Recreation Services

Recreation Services brings our community together through programs, events, and facilities. We manage the Consort SportEx (including the ice arena, curling rink, bowling alley, and community hall), sports grounds with ball diamonds and bunnock facilities, and two campgrounds for visitors and residents to enjoy.

**Contact**: rec@consort.ca

[Learn More →](/departments/recreation)

### Protective Services

The Protective Services department encompasses our Fire Department, Emergency Management team, and Bylaw Enforcement. Together, these services work to keep Consort safe, prepared for emergencies, and ensure community standards are maintained with fairness and respect.

**Fire Department**: fire@consort.ca  
**Emergency Management**: emergency.management@consort.ca  
**Bylaw Enforcement**: bylaw@consort.ca

[Learn More →](/departments/protective-services)

### Community Services

Community Services supports the well-being of residents of all ages by coordinating programs and services in partnership with local organizations. From seniors' programs and youth initiatives to volunteer opportunities and social support services, we help build a caring and inclusive community.

**FCSS**: fcssconsort@gmail.com  
**Learning & Community Centre**: neutralhillslearning@gmail.com

[Learn More →](/departments/community-services)

### Economic Development

Consort Economic Development works to support local businesses, attract new investment, and promote sustainable growth. Through initiatives like Return To Rural and partnerships that strengthen the local economy, we're helping Consort thrive as a place to live, work, and do business.

**Contact**: ecdev@consort.ca

[Learn More →](/departments/economic-development)
