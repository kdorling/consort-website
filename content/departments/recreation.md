The Recreation Services department brings energy and connection to our community through programs, events, and well-maintained facilities. We manage the Consort SportEx—home to the community hall, ice arena, curling rink, and bowling alley—as well as the Sports Grounds, which feature five ball diamonds, a bunnock facility, and a seasonal concession. Recreation Services is also responsible for both of the Village's campgrounds: the Consort Municipal Campground and the Open Sky Getaway. Whether you're looking to stay active, host an event, or enjoy the outdoors, we're here to help make it happen.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I book the community hall for a wedding?

Contact Recreation Services as early as possible (popular dates book up quickly). We'll provide rental information, rates, and an application form. A damage deposit is required.

### When does the ice go in at the arena?

Ice installation typically occurs in late September or early October, depending on weather. Check with the office for specific dates each year.

### Can I bring my RV for an extended stay at the campground?

Yes! Monthly rates are available at both campgrounds. Contact us for availability and rates.

### How do I register my child for hockey?

Registration typically opens in late August/early September. Watch for announcements or contact rec@consort.ca for registration dates and information.

### Is there public skating?

Yes! Public skating times are scheduled throughout the ice season. Check the arena schedule or contact the SportEx office for current times.

### Can non-residents rent facilities?

Yes, facilities are available to non-residents. Different rates may apply for residents versus non-residents.

### Do you offer birthday party packages?

Bowling birthday parties can be arranged. Contact rec@consort.ca for information and availability.
