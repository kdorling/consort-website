The Community Services department supports the well-being and vibrancy of Consort by working alongside local organizations to deliver programs and services for residents of all ages. We help coordinate and promote seniors' programs, youth initiatives, volunteer opportunities, and social support services offered throughout the community. Whether you're looking to get involved, find support, or connect with others, Community Services is here to help build a caring and inclusive village.

## What We Do

Community Services works in partnership with local organizations and agencies to:

- Coordinate and support social programs for all ages
- Connect residents with services and resources
- Promote volunteer opportunities
- Support community wellness initiatives
- Facilitate partnerships between organizations
- Advocate for community needs
- Build social connections and reduce isolation
- Enhance quality of life for all residents

## Frequently Asked Questions

### I want to volunteer. Where do I start?

Contact fcssconsort@gmail.com to discuss volunteer opportunities that match your interests and availability.

### Are there programs for seniors who are isolated?

Yes, FCSS offers programs specifically designed to reduce social isolation and support seniors. Contact fcssconsort@gmail.com to learn more.
