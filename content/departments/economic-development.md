## Return To Rural – Live. Work. Play.

The **Return To Rural** initiative promotes Consort and the surrounding region as an ideal place to live, work, and play. We're showcasing the advantages of rural living to individuals, families, and businesses looking to escape urban congestion and discover a better quality of life.

### Why Choose Rural Living in Consort?

**Affordable Living**:
- Lower cost of housing compared to urban centers
- Competitive property prices for residential and commercial development
- Reduced living expenses
- More space for your investment

**Quality of Life**:
- Safe, tight-knit community
- Clean air and wide-open spaces
- Short commutes and no traffic
- Access to outdoor recreation
- Family-friendly environment
- Strong sense of community

**Economic Opportunity**:
- Growing agricultural sector
- Support for entrepreneurs and small business
- Available commercial space
- Proximity to regional markets
- Access to skilled workforce
- Business-friendly environment

**Modern Amenities**:
- Reliable municipal services (water, sewer, utilities)
- High-speed internet availability
- Quality recreation facilities
- Healthcare services
- Educational opportunities
- Well-maintained infrastructure

### Remote Work in Consort

With the rise of remote work, Consort offers an ideal setting for professionals who can work from anywhere:
- Affordable home office space

- Peaceful work environment
- Better work-life balance
