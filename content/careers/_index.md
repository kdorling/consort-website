## How to Apply

### Application Process

1. **Review the Job Posting**: Carefully read the full job description and requirements
2. **Prepare Your Application**: Include a cover letter, resume, and any required documents
3. **Submit Your Application**: Email, mail, or deliver in person by the closing date
4. **Selection Process**: Qualified candidates will be contacted for interviews
5. **Reference Checks**: Final candidates will have references checked
6. **Job Offer**: The successful candidate will receive a formal offer of employment

### Application Submissions

**Email**: info@consort.ca  
Subject line: "Application - [Position Title]"

**Mail or In Person**:  
Village of Consort  
Attention: Human Resources  
[Address]  
Consort, AB [Postal Code]

**Office Hours**: Weekdays 8:00 AM to 4:30 PM

### What to Include in Your Application

- **Cover Letter**: Explain why you're interested in the position and how your skills and experience match the requirements
- **Resume/CV**: Include your education, work experience, skills, and relevant certifications
- **References**: Provide contact information for at least three professional references (or indicate "Available upon request")
- **Required Certifications**: Include copies of licenses, tickets, or certifications specified in the job posting
- **Proof of Education**: Copies of diplomas or degrees (if requested)

### Application Tips

- Submit your application before the closing date and time
- Tailor your cover letter to the specific position
- Highlight relevant experience and skills
- Proofread carefully for errors
- Follow any specific instructions in the job posting
- Ensure contact information is current and accurate

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I know if a position is still open?

Check this page for current postings. If a position is listed with a future closing date, it's still accepting applications.

### Can I apply for multiple positions?

Yes, you're welcome to apply for any positions for which you're qualified. Submit a separate application for each position.

### Do I need to live in Consort to work for the Village?

Residency in Consort is not required for most positions. However, some positions (like volunteer firefighters) require living within a certain response distance.

### When will I hear back about my application?

Only candidates selected for an interview will typically be contacted. This usually occurs within 2-3 weeks after the closing date.

### Does the Village offer casual or on-call positions?

Yes, we occasionally have casual and on-call positions, particularly in public works and recreation. These are posted as they become available.

### What is the dress code for interviews?

Business casual attire is appropriate for most positions. Dress professionally and comfortably.

### Can I volunteer while I look for employment?

Absolutely! Volunteering is a great way to get involved, gain experience, and become known in the community.
