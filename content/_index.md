Welcome to the Village of Consort, a thriving community in east-central Alberta where small-town charm meets big opportunities. Nestled in the heart of the prairies, Consort offers residents and visitors a welcoming atmosphere, strong community spirit, and access to excellent services and amenities.

## Discover Consort

The Village of Consort is home to a close-knit community that takes pride in its agricultural heritage, modern infrastructure, and commitment to quality of life. Whether you're a long-time resident, new to the area, or just passing through, you'll find a warm welcome and a place where people truly care about their neighbors.

## Our Community

- **Quality Services**: From reliable utilities to well-maintained roads and recreation facilities, we're committed to delivering excellent municipal services
- **Strong Economy**: With a focus on agriculture, small business, and economic development, Consort offers opportunities for growth and prosperity
- **Active Living**: Our recreation programs, sports facilities, and community events bring people together year-round
- **Safe & Secure**: Our dedicated fire department, emergency management team, and bylaw services keep our community safe

## What We Offer

Consort provides a full range of municipal services including water and sewer systems, waste collection, road maintenance, snow removal, and more. Our Recreation Services department manages the Consort SportEx—featuring an ice arena, curling rink, bowling alley, and community hall—as well as sports grounds with ball diamonds and two campgrounds for those looking to enjoy the great outdoors.

We're proud to support local businesses and welcome new investment through our Economic Development initiatives. With programs like Return To Rural, we're helping people discover the benefits of living, working, and playing in Consort.

## Get Involved

From volunteer opportunities to community programs for all ages, there are many ways to connect and contribute in Consort. Check out our Events calendar, explore our Recreation programs, or contact our Community Services department to learn more about what's happening in the village.

*The Village of Consort is located in Special Area No. 4 in east-central Alberta, approximately 260 kilometers southeast of Edmonton.*
