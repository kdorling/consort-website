Welcome to the Bank of Canada website. We are Canada's central bank, and our primary responsibility is to preserve the value of money by keeping inflation low, stable and predictable.

## Our Role

The Bank of Canada is the nation's central bank. We are not a commercial bank and do not offer banking services to the public. Rather, we have responsibilities for Canada's monetary policy, bank notes, financial system, and funds management.

## What We Do

- **Monetary Policy**: We conduct monetary policy to preserve the value of money by keeping inflation low, stable and predictable
- **Bank Notes**: We design, issue and distribute Canada's bank notes
- **Financial System**: We promote the safety, soundness and efficiency of Canada's financial system
- **Funds Management**: We provide funds management services and act as fiscal agent for the Government of Canada
